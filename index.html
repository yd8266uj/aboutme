About me

I am currently a computer science student at Metropolitan State University in Saint Paul. This is my second take at the whole college thing. I was initially an electrical engineering student at the University of Minnesota. I have worked on various software development projects over the past 10 years. I love learning about new technologies and finding new ways to deliver emerging software to everyday people.

I started off making webpages for friends and family in high school. Later, I moved on to developing electrical components for embedded systems on solar cars and, most recently, worked on developing vital technology in the regenerative medicine sector. I have worked on various smaller projects including phone apps, WordPress themes/plugins, and game mods.

When I'm not working or playing on my computer, my hobbies are cooking, RPGs (the kind with sheets of paper and dice) and riding my bike (the kind that you have to pedal).
